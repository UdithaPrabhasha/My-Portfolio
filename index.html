About me

I’m Uditha Prabhasha, an undergraduate student from Sri Lanka. 23 years old.Photography is my hobby and passion, and I do like to participate in photography competitions as well. I was awarded the 3rd place winner of the State Photography Festival Sri Lanka portfolio 2020.I'm also the winner of the Sri Lanka 2022 Sony World Photography Awards under the regional category. How I specialised in my photography is that I always wanted to create stories through my photographs.



In 2021 I have successfully held my very first photography exhibition " Untold Stories " which established many open doors to explore in my photography adventure. I’m thrilled to say that my very first interview article was published marking one of the milestones in my journey. All of you are welcome to read the interview article about my photography journey, my experiences in " Untold Stories " exhibition, and my future goals.
